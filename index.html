Pelé's achievements

Goalscorer

Pelé is the joint-top goalscorer for Brazil with 77 goals in 92 games. At the club level, he was Santos' all-time top goalscorer with 643 goals in 659 games. He scored a total of 1281 goals for club and country.

World Cup winner

Pelé first played in the World Cup at the age of 17. He played in 4 World cups and went on to win it 3 times, the only player to ever do so. He won it back to back in 1958 and 1962 and later in 1970.

Brazilian Champion

Pelé won the brazilian league 6 times with his club Santos FC. He did so 5 times in a row from 1961 to 1965. He then won it again in 1968.

National Treasure

In 1961, when Pelé was only 21 years old, Brazil’s president Janio Quadros declared Pelé a national treasure, preventing him from signing on with a team outside of Brazil.
