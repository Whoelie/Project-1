Pelé's timeline

1940
Edson Arantes do Nascimento (Pelé) is born in Três Corações, Brazil.
1956
Pelé makes his debut for Santos FC at age 15.
1958
Pelé becomes the youngest player to play in a World Cup at the age of 17. He helps Brazil win their first World Cup title, scoring two goals in the final against Sweden.
1962
Pelé scores in the World Cup final against Czechoslovakia, helping Brazil win their second consecutive title.
1970
Pelé leads Brazil to their third World Cup victory, scoring four goals in the tournament.
1971
Pelé scores his 1000th career goal.
1977
Pelé retires from professional football.
